About Jaycees Devchuli

JCI Nepal (नेपाल जेसीज) is a non-profit voluntary membership based organization of young active citizens age 18 to 40 working in Nepal since 1964 for developing the Leadership, Personality and Entrepreneurship skills of young people of Nepal. JCI Senator Dr Bhekh Bhadur Thapa is the founder President of JCI Nepal (नेपाल जेसीज).
